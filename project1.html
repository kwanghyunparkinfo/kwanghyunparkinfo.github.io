New Generation Databases and Data Empowering Technologies

New Generation Databases and Data Empowering Technologies
A3 Foresight Program, National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF)

November 1, 2025

By BDAI Lab

Project Duration: Agust 2025 ~ August 2029

Project Overview

• Build a next-generation unified database system for relational + tensor data integration
• Design for multimodal, cross-domain analytics across structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data
• Focus on core innovations including unified RA-LA data model, cloud-native & heterogeneous system architecture, and cross-engine query optimization
• Support secure, efficient, and scalable management of structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data
• Enable deep integration of data management and AI workflows for applications in science, industry, and national infrastructure

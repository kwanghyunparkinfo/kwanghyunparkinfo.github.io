Teaching

Our teaching spans undergraduate, graduate, and interdisciplinary courses across computer architecture, cloud systems, big data systems, object-oriented programming, and recent advances in AI systems.

Teaching Philosophy

BDAI Lab’s teaching emphasizes rigorous systems foundations, modern data and AI infrastructure, and hands-on problem solving. Courses are designed to connect core principles with emerging research themes in databases, cloud systems, AI systems, and computer architecture.
